This is a monthly shopping list organizer. Once user has all the items on their list, their monthly shopping is complete.

E.g. shopping_list <-- food & cleaning_supplies & self_care

Means in order to complete shopping list user must buy food, cleaning_supplies and self_care

These 3 items can further be divided into smaller sections. 
E.g. food <-- frozen & pantry & drinks & fruits & vegetables

To complete buying food, user must buy frozen, pantry, drinks, fruits and vegetables

To buy something, write the command "tell". One or multiple items can be bought at once. An entire group can also be bought at once. E.g. tell food - means you are buying all subcategories of food

User can add their own txt file according to their shopping needs. KB.txt is provided as an example.
